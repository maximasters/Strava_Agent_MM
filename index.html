💡 How it works

A training block consists of a 20-week period (140 days) ending on the date of your race.

Week 20 represents your race week, ending on the race day itself. All prior weeks are aligned relatively (Monday through Sunday) based on the race day-of-week.

Runs are filtered and aggregated automatically from your Strava activity history.
